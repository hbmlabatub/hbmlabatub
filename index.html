Human Behavior Modeling Lab is ab academic lab that is uses artificial intelligence to study and design algorithms for behavior modeling. Research topics that the lab is currently working on are sign language detection, pose estimation and language processing. The lab is a part of Computer Science and Engineering department at University at Buffalo. The aim lab aims to bridge the gap between the theoretical models and their real life applications to assist man kind.
